Privacy Policy for Polish Learner

Effective date: 2026-04-09

Polish Learner respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and why.

Data we collect

We may collect:

  • Email address used for sign-in
  • Password used for authentication
  • Lesson progress, streaks, XP, and sync status
  • Cloud sync data stored in Supabase
  • Basic app/device data needed to keep sync working

How we use data

We use this data to:

  • Create and sign in to your account
  • Sync your lesson progress across devices
  • Restore progress after reinstalling or switching devices
  • Keep the app working correctly

Sharing and storage

  • Data is stored in Supabase for cloud sync.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not share your data with third parties except as needed to operate the app and cloud sync.

Retention and deletion

Your progress is kept until you delete it, sign out, or request removal where supported by the service.

Children under 13

This app may be used by children under 13 with parent or guardian consent and supervision. We do not knowingly collect more personal data than is needed to provide the app and cloud sync features. If a child has provided personal data without the required consent, contact us so it can be removed.

Parents or guardians may contact us to review, update, or delete a child account's cloud data where applicable.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect data, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your choices

You can sign out of the cloud account, stop using cloud sync, or delete the app to remove local data on the device.
